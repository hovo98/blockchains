How does IBC’s interchain create value for the Cosmos Hub?

Many popular blockchains are plagued by problems like scaling issues, sky-high fees, and lengthy transaction times. Ethereum has already hit 99.15% capacity and is running out of space as well as providing a poor user experience.

IBC is a protocol that allows blockchains to talk to each other. That means being able to transfer tokens and other data frictionlessly and trustlessly between previously unconnected blockchains. 

For the Gravity Bridge,  IBC will enable the free flow of ERC20 tokens between Ethereum (and other EVM-enabled chains) to the Cosmos Hub as well as IBC tokens from Cosmos to Ethereum. This positions the Cosmos Hub at the center of interchain communication and exchange, expanding the ATOM's scope well beyond the Cosmos.

18.55 Million ATOM delegated to community focused Cosmos Hub Validators

Tendermint's re-delegration program review and subsequent execution was completed on April 30th. A total of 18.55 Million ATOM were delegated to 65 Cosmos Hub validators who have added tremendous value to the growth to the Cosmos Ecosystem. 

As the decision process entailed bucketing each validator into a tier based on the scope and cadence of their public goods contributions, we have delegated amounts from 100,000 ATOM to a max of 600,000 ATOM. Stay tuned for the next delegation round!
