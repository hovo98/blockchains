Cosmos Hub governance votes on cross-chain asset transfers

Last week, a governance proposal (prop#41) was put forth to enable transferring and receiving assets on the Cosmos Hub using IBC (Inter-Blockchain Communication). If passed and implemented, the Cosmos Hub would be the first blockchain ever able to accept and send assets to any IBC-enabled blockchain. This is truly the first step to universal blockchain interoperability.

The voting will continue until the 29th of March. If you're an ATOM delegator take a look to see if you validators voted.

Injective Protocol joins the Tendermint Fellows Program

The Tendermint Fellows program started last October to provide early stage financial backing to kickstart up and coming ideas and applications. The program welcomes a new fellow, Injective Protocol, to join legacy fellows Osmosis and Quasar.

Injective is building a decentralized derivatives exchange that enables the trading of limitless markets. The protocol’s native chain is using the Cosmos SDK and contributing extensively to the expansion of value in the Cosmos ecosystem.

Cosmos & IBC
Cosmos featured on “The Pomp Podcast”

Ethan Buchman, Interchain Foundation, Informal Systems and Cosmos co-founder, chatted about his views on how people can build technological systems that resemble biological sustainable systems. This ultimately led to his focus and work on blockchain, proof-of-stake, Tendermint and Cosmos. 

Cosmos, he describes, is architected to be a sustainable economic system based on the sovereignty and interoperability at its design's core. Ethan also stressed the importance of standardization and explains how the Inter-Blockchain Communication (IBC) protocol works to standardize cross-chain communication.

Cosmos Hub community showed their support for adding a DEX!

The Gravity DEX governance signal proposal (#prop38) to add liquidity mechanisms to the Cosmos Hub passed. That means that the Cosmos Hub community signaled their support for adding the Gravity DEX onto Cosmos Hub. 

The Gravity DEX will act as a marketplace for trading tokens from any connected network including tokens from Cosmos-SDK powered networks, Ethereum tokens including DeFi tokens and tokens from connected networks via IBC or Peg or others. 

The Gravity DEX opens the door to many new DeFi opportunities for the Cosmos Hub in its path forward to becoming a port city. This analogy used by Cosmos co-founder, Ethan Buchman, describes the evolution and purpose of the Hub. Watch this space for more news!

Agoric launches competitive testnet

Cosmos ecosystem contributor and project, Agoric, is launching their first incentivized testnet. The Agoric testnet is a series of global, competitive challenges for validators to prepare, learn, and earn points in advance of Agoric's mainnet.
