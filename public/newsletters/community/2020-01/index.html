An important milestone to look out for is the delivery of the Game of Zones demo. This will expand on the original IBC demo initially developed for the DeFi hackathon. Cosmos numbers snapshot

5162
Transactions
Transaction volumes over the last 30 days peaked at 5162 total transactions as of yesterday (1/15/20). Relative to stablecoin volumes, for instance, this is pretty anemic, but the result is not too surprising, as the ATOM is functioning in line with its stated utility—that of a staking token.
74%
Bonded or locked up
74.13% of the entire token supply of the network is currently bonded or locked up (~185M out of a total 250M ATOMs in circulation).
$4m
in ATOM bonded Dec 23
Cosmos continues to experience a strong staking ecosystem. Peak delegation activity from the last 30-day period was on Dec 23, 2019, where 909k ATOM (~$4m at today’s prices) were bonded just days before Christmas (source).

Figment Networks, a Cosmos Validator, is proposing the creation and administration of a Cosmos Governance Working Group. Make sure you weigh in on the proposal and voting.
