Cosmos Governance working group just release their parameter-change documentation for more fluid governance proposal coordination and resolution

Polychain Labs proposes a model of implementing threshold signatures to reduce exposure to risks like down time or double signing

Confio released the MASK, a new CosmWasm contract for validator key rotation and trading bonded ATOMS without changing the stacking module
